Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before renting a jet ski.

Jet ski rental prices at AquaLux start at $79 per hour depending on location and model. Our Discovery package begins at $89/hour for a Yamaha WaveRunner EX. Half-day rentals (4 hours) are $249 and full-day rentals (8 hours) are $399. All prices include fuel, safety equipment, and insurance.
Requirements vary by state. In Florida, riders born after January 1, 1988 must have a boating safety education ID card. In California, all operators need a California Boater Card. We provide a comprehensive safety briefing at every location, and our team can help you meet local requirements before your ride.
A personal watercraft, or PWC, is a recreational watercraft that the rider sits or stands on rather than inside. Popular brands include Yamaha WaveRunner, Sea-Doo, and Kawasaki Jet Ski. The term "jet ski" is commonly used to refer to all personal watercraft, though it is technically a Kawasaki brand name.
You must be at least 18 years old to rent a jet ski from AquaLux Rentals. Riders as young as 16 may operate a jet ski in some states when accompanied by a supervising adult on the same watercraft. Children can ride as passengers with an adult operator, and we provide youth-sized life jackets.
Bring sunscreen, sunglasses with a strap, a towel, and a change of clothes. We provide life jackets, safety equipment, and a complimentary waterproof phone pouch with Explorer and Odyssey packages. Wear swimwear or quick-dry clothing and water-friendly footwear. Leave valuables in your vehicle or use our secure lockers.
