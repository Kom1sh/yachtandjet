About This Destination

Cruise the Pacific Coast from San Diego Bay

San Diego is one of America's finest yacht charter destinations, offering year-round sunshine, calm bay waters, and stunning Pacific coastline. From the sheltered beauty of San Diego Bay to the dramatic La Jolla sea cliffs, every charter reveals a new dimension of Southern California's coastal splendor. Dolphins regularly surf alongside our vessels, and during winter months, migrating gray and humpback whales put on a breathtaking show just offshore.

Glide past the iconic Coronado Bridge, anchor off Coronado Island for a swim in crystal-clear water, and cruise north along the La Jolla coastline where sea caves and marine reserves create an unforgettable backdrop. As the sun sets over the Pacific, the sky ignites in colors you simply cannot experience from land. Whether you are celebrating a milestone, hosting clients, or seeking a perfect day on the water, San Diego delivers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Absolutely. San Diego is one of the premier whale-watching destinations on the West Coast. Gray whales migrate past San Diego from December through April, and humpback whales and blue whales are frequently spotted in summer and fall. Our captains know the best routes and will position the yacht for optimal viewing while maintaining safe, respectful distances from marine life. A yacht charter offers a far more intimate and comfortable whale-watching experience than crowded tour boats.
San Diego enjoys over 260 sunny days per year, making it an excellent year-round charter destination. Summer (June through September) offers the warmest water and longest days, ideal for swimming and extended sunset cruises. Fall and spring bring clear skies and lighter winds, perfect for smooth cruising. Winter is whale season, with water temperatures still comfortable for on-deck dining and Pacific Coast exploration. Our captains will help you plan the perfect itinerary no matter when you visit.
Yes, the classic San Diego Bay route takes you under the iconic Coronado Bridge, which stands over 200 feet above the water and offers a dramatic photo opportunity from the deck. From there, you can cruise around Coronado Island, anchor off the Silver Strand for swimming, or continue south past the naval base for unique military vessel sightings. Your captain will tailor the route based on your interests, weather, and charter duration.
Yes, fishing is available on select charters. San Diego is world-renowned for its sportfishing, with yellowtail, tuna, dorado, and calico bass all within reach of our charter routes. We can arrange fishing equipment and bait in advance. Please note that all guests participating in fishing must hold a valid California fishing license, which can be purchased online before your trip. Let us know at booking so we can prepare the right tackle and plan a route that maximizes your time on productive fishing grounds.
