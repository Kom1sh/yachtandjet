About This Location

Ride the Turquoise Waters of Biscayne Bay

Miami Beach is one of the most thrilling jet ski destinations in the United States. With warm, turquoise water year-round and iconic waterfront scenery, there is no better place to experience personal watercraft riding at its finest. Our Miami Beach jet ski rental base launches directly into Biscayne Bay, giving you immediate access to miles of open water and stunning coastal views.

Cruise past the celebrity mansions of Star Island, take in the glittering skyline of downtown Miami, and ride along the legendary South Beach coastline. Whether you are a first-time rider or a seasoned enthusiast, the calm, protected waters of Biscayne Bay offer ideal conditions for an unforgettable day on the water. After your ride, the vibrant nightlife and dining scene of South Beach is just steps from our dock.

Miami Beach FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about jet ski rentals in Miami Beach.

Miami is a year-round jet ski destination thanks to its subtropical climate. Water temperatures rarely drop below 72°F, even in winter. The peak season runs from November through April when humidity is lower and skies are clearest. Summer months are warmer but can bring afternoon thunderstorms, so morning rides are recommended from June through September. Our team monitors conditions daily and will advise you on the best time to ride.
Yes! Star Island is one of the most popular stops on our Biscayne Bay jet ski routes. You can ride around the island and take in the celebrity waterfront mansions from the water. Our Explorer and Odyssey packages include a guided route past Star Island, Fisher Island, and the Miami skyline. There are designated no-wake zones near the island that we observe for safety and respect for residents.
Safety always comes first at AquaLux Rentals. If weather conditions are unsafe for riding — such as thunderstorms, high winds, or rough seas — we will contact you in advance to reschedule at no additional cost. Light rain typically does not affect operations on Biscayne Bay. If conditions change during your ride, our on-water support team will guide you back safely. We offer full refunds or free rescheduling for any weather-related cancellations.
You must be at least 18 years old with a valid ID to rent a jet ski in Miami. In Florida, anyone born after January 1, 1988 must also hold a valid boating safety education card to operate a personal watercraft. Riders ages 16-17 may operate with an adult supervisor on the same watercraft. Children of any age can ride as passengers with an adult operator — we provide youth-sized life jackets for younger guests.
