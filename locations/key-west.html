About This Location

Discover the Crystal-Clear Waters of the Florida Keys

Key West offers some of the most spectacular jet ski riding in the entire country. Surrounded by crystal-clear waters with visibility reaching 40 feet or more, this island paradise at the southernmost point of the continental United States delivers an experience unlike any other. Our Key West jet ski rental base puts you minutes from mangrove islands, backcountry flats, and the vibrant coral reefs that make the Florida Keys a world-renowned destination.

Navigate through winding mangrove trails where the water glows emerald green, explore the shallow backcountry flats teeming with marine life, and ride out toward the only living coral barrier reef in North America. After your ride, head to Mallory Square for the legendary Key West sunset celebration. Whether you choose a guided tour or a self-guided adventure, jet skiing in Key West is an experience that stays with you forever.

Crystal-Clear Visibility

Ride over water so clear you can see the ocean floor — visibility often exceeds 30 to 40 feet in the Keys.

Mangrove Trails

Explore winding mangrove channels and backcountry flats that are only accessible by personal watercraft.

Reef Access

Ride to snorkeling spots near the only living coral barrier reef in the continental United States.

Sunset Rides

Time your ride to catch the world-famous Key West sunset from the water — a truly magical experience.

Key West FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about jet ski rentals in Key West.

Yes, our Explorer and Odyssey packages include guided routes that take you near the coral reef areas of the Florida Keys. While you cannot ride a jet ski directly over the protected reef, we bring you to designated snorkeling spots near the reef where you can anchor and explore. The Florida Keys National Marine Sanctuary has specific zones where personal watercraft are permitted, and our guides know every approved route.
Key West offers excellent jet ski conditions year-round, but the prime season is November through May when the weather is dry, humidity is lower, and the seas are typically calmer. Water temperatures remain above 70°F even in winter. Summer months (June through October) bring warmer water and longer days, though afternoon rain showers are common. Morning rides during summer offer calm water and beautiful light for photos.
Absolutely. Our guided Key West jet ski tours are among our most popular offerings. Experienced local guides lead you through the best mangrove channels, across the backcountry flats, and to prime snorkeling locations near the reef. Guided tours are included with Explorer and Odyssey packages. You can also add a guide to any Discovery rental for an additional fee. Tours typically cover 20-30 miles of Key West waterways.
The waters around Key West are teeming with marine life. It is common to spot dolphins riding alongside your wake, sea turtles surfacing near the mangroves, and rays gliding through the crystal-clear shallows. In the backcountry flats, you may see nurse sharks, tarpon, and countless species of tropical fish. During winter months, manatees occasionally visit the warmer waters near Key West. Our guides know the best spots for wildlife encounters.
Key West water temperatures are among the warmest in the continental United States year-round. In summer, water temperatures reach 85°F to 88°F. During winter, temperatures typically range from 70°F to 75°F. Even in the coolest months of January and February, the water feels comfortable for riding and swimming. This makes Key West one of the few jet ski destinations where you can comfortably ride in shorts and a t-shirt every month of the year.
Yes, snorkeling stops are one of the highlights of jet skiing in Key West. Our Explorer and Odyssey packages include guided stops at prime snorkeling locations where you can anchor your jet ski and explore the underwater world. Snorkeling gear is available for rent at our dock, or you can bring your own. The crystal-clear visibility in the Keys makes these stops truly unforgettable — expect to see colorful tropical fish, sea fans, and vibrant coral formations.
