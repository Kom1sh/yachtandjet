Founded in 2018

Redefining Watercraft Rentals

AquaLux Rentals was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a premium experience on the water. What started as a two-jet-ski operation on Miami Beach has grown into a multi-location watercraft rental company serving thousands of guests each year across America's most beautiful coastlines.

Our founder, a lifelong waterman and former competitive jet ski racer, saw an industry that was often impersonal, inconsistent, and underwhelming. AquaLux was created to change that — to bring the same level of care, quality, and service to watercraft rentals that guests expect from a five-star hotel.

Our Values

What We Stand For

⚖

Safety Without Compromise

Every watercraft is inspected daily. Every guest receives a thorough briefing. Our on-water support team is always nearby. USCG compliant, fully insured, and uncompromising on safety protocols.

★

Fleet Excellence

We only operate current-year models from Yamaha, Sea-Doo, Azimut, Sunseeker, and Princess. Regular maintenance schedules ensure every watercraft performs at its best, every ride.

♢

Genuine Hospitality

Our team members are passionate water enthusiasts, not just operators. We share local knowledge, suggest the best routes, and go out of our way to make every experience exceptional.

◆

Environmental Responsibility

We operate in some of the world's most pristine waterways and take that responsibility seriously. Low-emission watercraft, reef-safe practices, and partnerships with marine conservation organizations.

Our Team

People Who Live on the Water

Every AquaLux team member is a certified water safety professional with genuine passion for the waterways they serve. Our captains hold USCG licenses. Our dock staff are trained in first aid and customer hospitality. And our fleet technicians are factory-certified mechanics.

When you rent from AquaLux, you're not just getting a jet ski or a yacht — you're getting a team of people who care deeply about your safety, your experience, and the waters we all share.
