Yacht Rental Questions

Every AquaLux yacht charter includes an experienced captain, fuel, insurance, basic amenities (towels, ice, cooler, Bluetooth sound system), and water toys (paddleboards, snorkeling gear, floats). Catering, premium beverages, and additional entertainment can be arranged for an extra fee.
We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance for weekday charters and 4-8 weeks for weekends and holidays. Peak season (June through September) and special dates like Fourth of July and Labor Day fill up quickly. Contact us early to secure your preferred yacht and date.
Yes, you're welcome to bring your own food and beverages aboard. We provide coolers and ice. Alternatively, we partner with local catering companies who specialize in yacht dining — from gourmet charcuterie boards to full multi-course meals prepared on board.
Safety is our top priority. If conditions are deemed unsafe by our captain, we offer a full reschedule at no charge. Light rain typically doesn't affect charters — our yachts have enclosed cabins and covered flybridge areas. We monitor weather closely and will contact you 24 hours in advance if there are concerns.
